About Bacoor City

Know more about our rich history and vision for the future

Our History

Bacoor, officially known as the City of Bacoor, is a first-class component city in the province of Cavite, Philippines. According to the 2020 census, it has a population of 664,625 people, making it the most populous city in the province.

The city was created through Republic Act No. 9017, signed by President Gloria Macapagal Arroyo on March 15, 2002. However, Bacoor's history dates back to the Spanish colonial period, playing a significant role in the Philippine Revolution.
